Cookies policy

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored in an internet browser when you visit almost any web page. Its usefulness is that the web is capable of remembering your visit when you return to that page. Cookies usually store information of a technical nature, personal preferences, personalization of content, usage statistics, access to user accounts, etc. The purpose of the cookie is to adapt the content of the web to your profile and needs. How to use and delete cookies?

Most web browsers accept cookies, but you can change the settings of the web browser to reject new cookies, disable those that already exist or notify you when someone sends you a new cookie on the device. To change the configuration, follow the instructions provided for each browser:

  • Internet Explorer: Tools > Internet Options > Privacy.

    How to delete cookies on Internet Explorer

  • Firefox: Tools > Options > Privacy > History > Custom settings.

    How to delete cookies on Mozilla Firefox

  • Chrome: Settings > Show advanced settings > Privacy > Content settings.

    How to delete cookies on Google Chrome

  • Safari: Preferences > Security.

    How to delete cookies on Safari

  • Opera: Settings.

    How to delete cookies on Opera

Blocking the use of cookies in your browser may make some services or features of the website unavailable.
