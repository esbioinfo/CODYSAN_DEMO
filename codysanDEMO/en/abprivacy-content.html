What information we do NOT collect?

The data provided in our diagnostic tool are not stored. This information is only used to provide an indicative diagnosis and does not connect to any database. Nor do we store the data used in the contact section of our website.

How do we use the information from the diagnostic tool?

The information provided by the user for the diagnostic tool is used by the algorithm to check which conditions are met and to make a first diagnosis of congenital dyserythropoietic anemia. At no time are the user's name or contact information required. You can check the operation of the algorithm here.
